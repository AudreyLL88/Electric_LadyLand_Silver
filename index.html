Jewelry Ethics
Beauty is all around us and we would like everybody to notice! We want to help people to discover their inner creativity through being close to what nature has to offer. Silver and natural gems are ancient and beloved products that deserve care and respect. We believe that the art of smithery can help you re-discover your abilities while having fun!

Your Instructor
After a revealing discovery while following the footsteps of her lost brother in Nebraska, Matildha studied with passion the art of silversmithing in Sweden as well as in India. She turned that passion into her work when she decided to create Electric LadyLand Silver,a one woman ran operation with the strive to keep “the old ways alive”. Her inspiration comes from Native American culture, nature, vintage finds and rock'n'roll.
